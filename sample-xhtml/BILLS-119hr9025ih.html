119th CONGRESS
2d Session
H. R. 9025


To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 200 1st Avenue Southeast in Austin, Minnesota, as the “John Madden Memorial Post Office”.

IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES

May 26, 2026

Mr. Finstad (for himself, Mr. Emmer, Mr. Stauber, Mrs. Fischbach, Ms. Morrison, Ms. Craig, and Ms. McCollum) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform

A BILL

To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 200 1st Avenue Southeast in Austin, Minnesota, as the “John Madden Memorial Post Office”.

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. John Madden Memorial Post Office.

(a) Designation.—The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 200 1st Avenue Southeast in Austin, Minnesota, shall be known and designated as the “John Madden Memorial Post Office”.

(b) References.—Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the “John Madden Memorial Post Office”.
